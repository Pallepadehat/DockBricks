# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want DockBricks to be a welcoming, respectful project for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation, not people.
- Share context when reporting bugs or proposing changes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks, insults, or sustained hostility.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Disruptive behavior that harms collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct.
Repeated or severe violations may lead to warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent removal from community spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private security advisory:
`https://github.com/pallepadehat/DockBricks/security/advisories/new`
